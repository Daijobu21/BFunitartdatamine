Episode 6
—Premonitions—


From this vantage point, the sky and the sea seem joined as one in the distant horizon.

Tevarius narrows his golden eyes at the gathering clouds as the faint smell of wet earth greets his senses.

Tevarius
(The storms...have grown more frequent.)

Tevarius
(Just like when I was roused from my slumber in the Eastern Plains...)

Sero-Anya
Teeeevvv!

Sero-Anya
Whatcha looking at?

Tevarius startles and looks down at the child bounding up next to him, arms full of picked flowers.

Tevarius
The...The sky.

Tevarius
It's, um, a nice color, isn't it?

Sero-Anya
You sure like the sky, don't you Tev? You keep staring at it!

Sero-Anya
Can you fly? Are you actually a winged creature of some kind?

Tevarius
Um...No?

Tevarius
(Ahh, my friend...How did it come to this?)

Sero-Anya
Hm~Hmm~

Sero-Anya
Oh! Someone's coming up the road...

Tevarius
Juno...?

Tevarius
No, but...

Tevarius
You look so much like her, but you're a...child!

Tevarius
Ahhh, my friend! What...How...?

Sero-Anya
Umm...I'm Sero-Anya...

Sero-Anya
Are you looking for someone?

Sero-Anya watches the strange, pointy-eared man in lavish garb pace up and down the dirt path, one hand massaging his temple.

Tevarius
Really! This is...absurd! What am I to do with a...a goddess-child?!

Tevarius
How am I supposed to help?

Sero-Anya
Ummm...

Sero-Anya
I-I'm going to leave now, if you don't mind...

Sero-Anya takes a few paces backwards, then breaks into a small jog away from the man...

...and promptly trips on a small stone marker.

Sero-Anya
Eeeek!

Sero-Anya
O-Oh...

Sero-Anya finds herself suspended in midair by a pair of sturdy arms.

Tevarius
Whew. You should be more careful!

Sero-Anya
(This man...feels familiar...)

Sero-Anya
(Somehow I feel...at ease...)

Sero-Anya
Umm...Thank you.

Tevarius
I'm glad you're safe. I've come in search of you, actually, at the behest of Great Sage Zeruiah.

Tevarius
My name is Tevarius.

Sero-Anya
Tevar...Teh-vah...ree-us...

Sero-Anya
...Can I call you Tev? Or Tevvy?

Tevarius
...Sure.

Sero-Anya
Did you mention Zeru just now? I dreamed about her, you know!

Sero-Anya
I was just going to look for her!

Tevarius
I see...

Tevarius
In that case, we'd best be on our way.

Tevarius
This is no place for a lone child.

Sero-Anya
Hey...can I hold your hand, Tev? So I don't trip again?

Tevarius
... ...

Tevarius
Hold on tight.

Tevarius
(This is quite a predicament...)

Tevarius
(It will be a trial to bring her back to Zeruiah, with the Vale in such a state.)

Tevarius
(It's a miracle I even found her alive and well, what with all these vile creatures prowling the land—)

Sero-Anya
Tevvy? You're making a funny face...

Tevarius sighs, and places a warm hand on Sero-Anya's shoulder.

Tevarius
It's nothing.

Glancing out over the horizon and back at her, Tevarius kneels and looks into Sero-Anya's eyes.

Tevarius
Sero-Anya, do you remember anything about the people in your dreams?

Tevarius
The two ladies you've been telling me about.

Sero-Anya avoids his gaze and fiddles with her hair.

Sero-Anya
Not really...

Sero-Anya
I mean, I think I...know them, but I don't know their names.

Sero-Anya
I don't know why they're fighting, but at the same time I kind of do.

Sero-Anya
...Sorry...That doesn't make any sense, does it?

Tevarius
Hmm...

Tevarius
You said someone told you to go to Zeruiah, yes?

Sero-Anya
Uh huh. A really soft and gentle voice.

Sero-Anya
Are we going to go look for her?

Tevarius
...Yes. I think we should.

Tevarius
Listen carefully, Sero-Anya.

Tevarius
Our journey will be dangerous, so it is imperative you stay close to me, and out of sight if you must.

Tevarius
We've been lucky so far, but where we're going, there's bound to be enemies.

Tevarius
I will do everything I can to keep you safe. You have my word.

Sero-Anya
O-Okay.

Sero-Anya
Tev, I don't want you to get hurt because of me. I've already...

Sero-Anya starts to sniffle.

Sero-Anya
I did...bad things to Zeru...

Sero-Anya
Some part of me feels like I've done it before.

Sero-Anya
But...But I'd never do such a thing...

Sero-Anya
That's just cruel and mean.

Tevarius
...It was just a dream, Sero-Anya. I'm sure Zeruiah is fine.

Tevarius
Let's be on our way and not keep her waiting, hmm?

Sero-Anya
Okay.

Tevarius watches Sero-Anya closely as she leaves the flowers by a makeshift stack of stones.

Tevarius
...Heavens above. Zeruiah, I do hope you're safe.

Tevarius
For all of our sakes.
