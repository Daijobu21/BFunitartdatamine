Centuries of being a living barrier had bolstered her confidence, but nevertheless, she was grateful for the threads Fate wove for her.

Her old friend, Zeruiah, was right in one thing, at least—that the foreign goddess was a creature of benevolence, and so was she.

And yet, the reincarnation she encountered before as a trembling waif was now brimming with something decidedly malicious.

Sparing no thought to that suspicious veneer of civility would be a disaster...
