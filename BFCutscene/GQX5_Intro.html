"Light and darkness are inseparable.""True of worlds beyond a mere mortal's comprehension,but true even of mere mortals themselves.""And when that light seeks to run from itself...""...darkness will always, always follow."


Aimyhr was a land formed from the coalescence of powerful magic.

Its many inhabitants lived in relative peace for thousands of years,

until one day, an otherworldly visitor sought refuge from a ravaging force that consumed her home.

Juno-Seto was her name.

A goddess of light, she had sacrificed herself to ensure her people's escape.

The great Starsage, Zeruiah, gave them shelter in the Vale,

where the Divine Seed the refugees brought with them was able to flourish and bloom.

Thus was Juno-Seto reborn in this land...

...but little did she know that her shadow would chase her even across worlds.

The Voidbringer, Ensa-Taya, and her army of darkness swept across Aimyhr,

tearing a wound through the Vale in pursuit of the radiant goddess who had left for the East.

When the two halves locked eyes at last, both knew that they were one and the same.

It was here that they fought to their last breaths,

whereupon Juno-Seto wrapped her hollowed shell, Ensa-Taya, in a fatal embrace.

The Crystalline Spire that encases the two goddesses stand in the Eastern Plains to this day...

But the lingering Void was not the only malevolent force lurking behind every shadow.

With Aimyhr so weakened by the invasion,

an antediluvian alien entity rose from a deep slumber to seize control of the surface world.

Her high priest, Saerenthis, saw a chance to drown the land for the Abyss,

and the Naga swarmed forth into the Vale to claim their first victory over the land-walkers.

However, they were repelled by the awakened dragon spirit Tevarius, in defense of his land.

The lingering spirit of Juno-Seto still yet looks out for the inhabitants of this world,

but her power has greatly diminished since; barely a whisper to be heard.

The myriad conflicts the world had seen in so short a time was troubling,

and it was clear that dark times were ahead for Aimyhr...

Nestled far from the conflicts lay the plains of the Southern Plateau.

It is here, in a sleepy village yet untouched by the Void,

where Sero-Anya stirs in her sleep, haunted by memories of forgotten lives.

It is here, that manifold destinies begin to converge into a singular point...
